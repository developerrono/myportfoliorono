IT Support: Network Infrastructure Upgrade

A case study on improving network reliability and performance for a small business.

My Role

IT Support Specialist

Client

Fictional Business Inc.

Duration

4 Weeks

The Challenge

The client was experiencing frequent network outages and slow internet speeds, which severely impacted their daily operations and productivity. The existing network hardware was outdated and unable to handle the increasing demands of the business. The main challenge was to diagnose the root cause of the issues and implement a comprehensive solution with minimal downtime.

My Process

1. Diagnostics

Conducted a full network assessment to identify bottlenecks and hardware failures.

2. Planning

Designed a new network topology and selected appropriate hardware for the upgrade.

3. Implementation

Installed new routers, switches, and access points, and configured network settings.

4. Support

Provided post-implementation support and training to staff to ensure a smooth transition.

The Solution

The solution involved replacing the old router with a modern, high-performance model and installing new managed switches to improve network traffic management. We also implemented a new firewall and intrusion detection system to enhance security. The entire process was carefully planned to occur after business hours, minimizing disruption to the client's operations.

The Results

The network upgrade resulted in a **90% reduction in reported network issues** and a significant increase in overall network speed. The client reported a noticeable improvement in productivity and a high level of satisfaction with the new infrastructure.
