UI/UX Case Study: E-commerce Redesign

A comprehensive case study on redesigning a mobile e-commerce application to improve user experience and conversion rates.

Role

Lead UI/UX Designer, Researcher

Client

Fictional Co.

Duration

3 Months

The Challenge

The existing e-commerce application suffered from a high rate of cart abandonment and negative user feedback related to a confusing checkout process and outdated interface. Our goal was to identify key pain points and create a more intuitive and visually appealing experience that would increase user satisfaction and boost sales.

My Process

1. Discover

User interviews, competitive analysis, and stakeholder workshops.

2. Define

Persona creation, user journey mapping, and problem statement definition.

3. Design

Wireframing, prototyping, and high-fidelity mockups in Figma.

4. Deliver

Usability testing, iteration, and preparation of design handoff assets.

The Solution

Based on our research, the solution involved simplifying the navigation, modernizing the visual design, and streamlining the checkout process into a single, clean user flow. Below are some of the key design screens.

The Results

Following the redesign, usability testing showed a **40% reduction in checkout time** and a **25% increase in task completion rates**. The new interface received positive feedback from users and stakeholders, paving the way for a successful product launch.
